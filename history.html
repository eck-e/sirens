History & Mission

The Penn Sirens was founded in 2011 with the encouragement of the Penn Women's Center and Harnwell College House's Dean, Dr. Suhnne Ahn. Since its inception, the Penn Sirens and its network of early support endeavor to give young women at the University of Pennsylvania the same opportunities for artistic expression, performance excellence and leadership, as well as musical community that is already afforded to young men on campus by the University of Pennsylvania Glee Club. Currently, no such organization exists on campus which affords the same to young women. The Penn Sirens continue to grow as an organization, maintaining close ties with its brother group, the Penn Glee Club. The Penn Sirens aspire to give its musicians and technical members every opportunity to share their talents with communities both near and far.
